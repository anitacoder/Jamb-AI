UTME  
(i) JOS requires five (5) 'O' level credit passes to include 
English Language and Mathematics. TC II merit in 
English and NCE General English are taken as 
equivalents of 'O' level credit pass in English Language. 
Candidates without credit pass in ‘O’ level 
Mathematics may be admitted but must either 
register and pass remedial Mathematics or 'O' level 
credit pass in Mathematics before graduation.  
(ii) IBADAN requires five (5) 'O' level credit passes at one 
(1) sitting including English Language and four (4) 
other subjects from Arts, Social Sciences and Sciences.  
(iii) KWASU requires 'O' level credit passes in English 
Language, Mathematics, any one (1) of Health Science, 
Biology, Chemistry or Physics, plus two (2) of 
Geography, Economics or Government.

 (i)  KWASU requires Mathematics plus other two (2)
