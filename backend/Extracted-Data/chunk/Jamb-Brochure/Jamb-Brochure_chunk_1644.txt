AUCHIPOLY, IBD -POLY, LASPOTECH, 
OSUNTECH, KWA-POLY, FEDPO-OKO and  
FEDPONEK require ‘O’ level credit pass in 
English Language.  
FEDPO-ADO and KAD-POLY accept Woodwork, 
Literature in English, Metal Work and Accounts 
as course requirements. FEDPO -ADO does not 
accept Metal Work.  
YABATECH requires five (5) ‘O’ level credit 
passes at not more than two (2) sittings which 
must include Ma thematics, English Language, 
Economics or Geography, Physics/ Chemistry 
and any from the following: Technical Drawing, 
Financial Accounting, Biology/Agricultural 
Science, Fine Art, Wood Work, Literature in  
English,  Metal  Work,  Principle  of  
Accounts/Bookkeeping, Government.  
IBD-POLY requires five (5) ‘O’ level credit passes 
at not more than two (2) sittings. Also requires ‘O’ 
level credit pass in Economics. It accepts 
Commerce, Basic Surveying and Agricultural 
Science as part of course requirements.
