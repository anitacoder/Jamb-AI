UTME  
(i) JOS requires five (5) 'O' level credit passes to include 
Mathematics, English Language and Biology.  
(ii) UNN requires 'O' level credit passes in English Language, 
Biology or Health Science, and any other three (3) subjects at 
least a pass in Mathematics or Statistics.  
(iii) IBADAN accepts Science subjects with a pass in ‘O’ level 
Mathematics.  
(iv) OAU requires five (5 ) 'O' level credit passes in English 
Language, Mathematics, Biology and any two (2) from 
Geography, Economics, Government, Chemistry, Physics, 
History, Literature in English, Yoruba, Christian Religious 
Studies /Islamic Studies, Agricultural Science, ICT, Civic 
Education.  
(v) UYO and IMSU require 'O' level credit passes in English 
Language, Biology/Health Science plus a pass in Mathematics. 
UYO requires a credit pass in Biology or Health Science and at 
least a D7 pass in Mathematics.
