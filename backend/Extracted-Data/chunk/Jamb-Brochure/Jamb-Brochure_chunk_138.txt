DIRECT ENTRY  
  In addition to having five (5) credit passes in SSC or its equivalent   at not more than two (2) sittings, candidates must possess at   least 
two (2) 'A' Level passes in subject relevant to the desired  programme. Holders of ND/NCE qualifications, with at least   Upper Credit or a CGPA of 2.5 
and above may be considered.  Otherwise the candidate is admitted into 100 level. HND holders   with  Upper Credit may be considered for 
300 level. Holders of   JUPEB certificate, with a minimum of 50% score from the   cumulative of 'O' level result (60%) and JUPEB result (40%), are  
 admitted into 200 level, otherwise the candidate is admitted into   100 level. NCE holders from the School of Ecumenical Education  
 with CGPA of 2.5 and above are admitted into 300 level of the  Faculty of Education, provided UTME requirements are met.
