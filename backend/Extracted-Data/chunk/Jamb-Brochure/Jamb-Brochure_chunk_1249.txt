FUTO requires  two (2) 'A' level passes not below Grade C in 
Chemistry and one (1) of Physics or Biology.  It accepts 
ND/HND upper credit in Biochemistry, Biotechnology, 
Biology, Microbiology, Science Laboratory Technology, 
Physics and Geology.  Candidates must in addition meet the 
UTME requirements.  It does not accept First Degree and 
NCE.
