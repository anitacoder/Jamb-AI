 2.2.07  FEDERAL UNIVERSITIES OF AGRICULTURE,  ABEOKUTA  
  
University of Agriculture, Abeokuta requires all candidates seeking admission into any of its programmes to possess at least five (5) SSC credit passes in relevant 
subjects including English Language and Mathematics at not more than two sittings.      
  
 The candidates must be sixteen (16) years of age by 1st of October in the year of admission.
