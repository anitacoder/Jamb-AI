DIRECT ENTRY ADMISSIONS  
  
Direct Entry candidates must also satisfy UTME (i.e O' Level) requirements in addition to possession of the following:  
  
1. Two (2) 'A' level passes in relevant subjects-  Placement at 200 Level  
2. ND, HND, NCE  
a) ND  at Upper credit    -  Placement at 200 level  
b) HND at Upper Credit    -  Placement at 300 Level  
c) HND at Lower Credit    -  Placement at 200 Level  
d) NCE at Distinction     -  Placement at 200 Level  
  
  
2.2.29 REDEEMER'S UNIVERSITY, EDE, OSUN STATE  
  
GENERAL ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS  
  
(i) Five (5) 'O' level credit passes in at least one sitting or six (6) 'O' level credit passes at not more than two (2) sittings.  
  
(ii) 'O' level credit passes in SSC or its equivalent in relevant subjects.   
  
(iii) 'O' level credit passes in Mathematics and English Language are required for all programmes in the Colleges of Natural Sciences and Management Sciences.
